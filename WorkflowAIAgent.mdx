## Overview

Supervity is a no-code AI automation platform that empowers teams to build intelligent agents that automate tasks, extract insights, and enhance productivity across enterprise operations. Whether you're automating repetitive tasks, enabling AI-powered customer support, or mining organizational knowledge, Supervity provides a secure and scalable platform to bring agentic intelligence into your workflows.

## Introduction

Supervity enables you to:

- **Create AI Agents** tailored to your team or organization’s specific needs.
- **Automate workflows** using advanced AI-driven logic, without writing a single line of code.
- **Integrate with enterprise tools** such as Slack, Salesforce, SAP, Google Workspace, and more.
- **Leverage modular agent templates** such as Knowledge AI Agents, Workflow Agents, Robotic Process Agents, and Process Discovery Agents.
- **Enable AI copilots** that can interact via chat, voice, or embedded walkthroughs in your apps.

Supervity combines the flexibility of AI with robust enterprise-grade controls — making it suitable for businesses of all sizes, across industries like finance, healthcare, retail, government, and more.

## Who is this for?

This documentation is designed for:

- **Business users** looking to automate workflows without engineering help
- **IT and Ops teams** seeking secure and scalable AI automation
- **Product and support teams** looking to deploy knowledge bots or customer-facing AI agents
- **Developers** wanting to customize, extend, and integrate Supervity with existing systems
